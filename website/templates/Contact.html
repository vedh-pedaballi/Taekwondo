Frequently Asked Questions

What are your class schedules?

We offer classes throughout the day, from early morning to evening. Check our Classes page for the complete schedule.

Do you offer trial classes?

Yes! We offer a free trial class for new students. Contact us to schedule your trial session.

What equipment do I need?

For most classes, comfortable workout clothes are sufficient. We provide all necessary equipment and safety gear.

Are there age restrictions?

We have programs for all ages, from children (4+) to adults. Each program is tailored to the appropriate age group.

Do you offer private lessons?

Yes, we offer private and semi-private lessons. Contact us for pricing and availability.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 24-hour notice for class cancellations. Monthly memberships can be paused with advance notice.
